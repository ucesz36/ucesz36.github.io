Hypotheses

  • Hypothesis 1 (H1): There exists a statistically and economically significant Chinese Lunar New Year (CLNY) holiday effect in the A-share market, both before and after the holiday period.
  • Hypothesis 2 (H2): The A-share market exhibits adaptive CLNY holiday effects, with the magnitude and pattern of the effect evolving over the past two decades.
  • Hypothesis 3 (H3): The outbreak of COVID-19 has altered the dynamic evolution of the adaptive CLNY holiday effect in the Chinese A-share market.
